Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Nairobi

Arrive at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport. Meet your guide and transfer to your hotel. Safari briefing and overnight stay. Meals: Dinner.

Day 2: Nairobi to Maasai Mara

Drive through the Great Rift Valley to Maasai Mara. Afternoon game drive. Overnight at a luxury tented camp. Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 3: Maasai Mara Game Drives

Full day in Maasai Mara with morning and afternoon game drives. Spot lions, cheetahs, elephants, and more. Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 4: Maasai Village Visit & Wildlife

Visit a Maasai village for cultural immersion. Optional hot air balloon safari (extra). Evening game drive. Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 5: Return to Nairobi & Departure

Morning game drive. Return to Nairobi for lunch and shopping before airport drop-off. Meals: Breakfast, Lunch.
