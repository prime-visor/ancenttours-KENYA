Lamu Island Retreat

Discover Serenity, Culture & Swahili Charm on Kenya’s Magical Coast.

4 Days Lamu Experience
Kenya Beach Holidays

Why Visit Lamu?

Pristine Beaches
Relax on endless white sands and swim in the warm Indian Ocean.
Dhow Cruises
Sail at sunset on a traditional dhow and explore the archipelago.
Swahili Culture
Wander Lamu Old Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and discover rich Swahili heritage.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Lamu

Fly from Nairobi to Lamu. Meet & greet at the airport, transfer by boat to your hotel. Afternoon at leisure to explore the beach or relax. Meals: Dinner.

Day 2: Lamu Old Town & Culture

Guided walking tour of Lamu Old Town (UNESCO site), visit museums, markets, and mosques. Afternoon free for shopping or beach time. Meals: Breakfast, Dinner.

Day 3: Dhow Cruise & Island Hopping

Morning dhow cruise to Manda or Shela Island, swimming and snorkeling. Sunset dhow cruise with Swahili snacks. Meals: Breakfast, Dinner.

Day 4: Departure

Breakfast at the hotel. Transfer by boat to the airport for your flight back to Nairobi. Meals: Breakfast.
